About Eagle Arena

Eagle arena is a game filled with fantastic battle ready Eagles, where players can collect them as pets. Players may use the Eagles to breed, raise, hunt and battle other players to earn rewards.

The players will be playing each other with mind games, always predicting the opponent's next move, reading each other's minds to counter one another to be victorious and reach the top rankings to be rewarded with the coveted resources.
